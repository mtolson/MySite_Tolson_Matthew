The Chorus

The chorus started back in 2003 as a group of men that got together to sing for fun. More and more men have joined the chorus over the years. The mission of the chorus is: We sing to inspire, encourage, and sustain excellence in the choral arts and to achieve a gay-affirming presence in the greater Miami Valley
